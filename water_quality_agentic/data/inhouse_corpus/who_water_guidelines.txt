WHO Drinking Water Guidelines – Simplified Excerpt
世界卫生组织（WHO）饮用水水质指南 – 精简摘录

1. Nitrate / 硝酸盐
- Recommended maximum concentration: 50 mg/L as NO₃⁻
- Long-term exposure above this level can increase health risks such as methemoglobinemia in infants.
- 建议最高浓度：以 NO₃⁻ 计 50 mg/L
- 长期超过该水平可能增加婴幼儿高铁血红蛋白症等健康风险。

2. Microbial Quality / 微生物水质
- E. coli: 0 CFU per 100 mL (should not be detectable in any drinking water sample).
- Presence of E. coli indicates recent fecal contamination and potential presence of pathogens.
- 大肠杆菌：100 mL 水样中不得检出（0 CFU）。
- 检出大肠杆菌说明存在粪便污染风险，以及其他病原体可能存在。

3. Heavy Metals / 重金属
- Lead (Pb): guideline value < 0.01 mg/L
- Arsenic (As): guideline value < 0.01 mg/L
- Cadmium (Cd): guideline value < 0.003 mg/L
- 铅（Pb）：指南值 < 0.01 mg/L
- 砷（As）：指南值 < 0.01 mg/L
- 镉（Cd）：指南值 < 0.003 mg/L

4. General Principles / 一般原则
- Regular monitoring of key parameters such as nitrate, microbial indicators, and heavy metals is essential.
- Protection of source water is the first barrier in a multi-barrier approach.
- 需要定期监测硝酸盐、微生物指标、重金属等关键参数。
- 保护水源地是多重防护体系中的第一道关键屏障。

5. Interpretation / 解释说明
- Values below the guideline are generally considered safe for lifetime consumption.
- Short-term peaks over the limit may still be acceptable if infrequent, but require investigation and management.
- 指标长期低于指南值通常被认为对终生饮用是安全的。
- 若短期监测值超过限值，需视频率与程度而定，通常需要进一步调查和管理措施。
