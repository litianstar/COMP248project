General Background – Lake Ontario and Drinking Water
总体背景 – 安大略湖与饮用水

--------------------------------------------------
1. Lake Ontario – Basic Facts
安大略湖 – 基本情况
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Lake Ontario is one of the Laurentian Great Lakes in North America.
- It receives inflows from Lake Erie via the Niagara River and drains into the St. Lawrence River.
- The lake supports multiple uses, including drinking water supply, recreation, fisheries, and shipping.

中文：
- 安大略湖是北美五大湖之一。
- 它通过尼亚加拉河接收来自伊利湖的水量，并最终经圣劳伦斯河向大西洋排水。
- 安大略湖具有多种用途，包括饮用水供给、休闲娱乐、渔业和航运等。

--------------------------------------------------
2. Drinking Water and Human Use
饮用水与人类利用
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Many communities around Lake Ontario rely on the lake as a source of treated drinking water.
- Intakes are usually located offshore and draw water that is later treated (e.g., filtration, disinfection).
- Ensuring compliance with WHO and local drinking water standards is essential for public health.

中文：
- 安大略湖周边的许多社区将湖水作为处理后饮用水的水源。
- 取水口通常位于离岸一定距离的水域，取水后经过过滤、消毒等工艺处理。
- 确保饮用水符合 WHO 以及当地标准，对公共健康至关重要。

--------------------------------------------------
3. Key Water-Quality Concerns
主要水质关注点
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Nutrients: nitrate and phosphorus from agricultural runoff, wastewater, and urban stormwater.
- Heavy metals: arsenic, cadmium, lead, and mercury from natural geology and human activities.
- Microbial contamination: E. coli and other pathogens from sewage, septic systems, and animal waste.
- Emerging concerns: microplastics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products.

中文：
- 营养盐：硝酸盐和磷主要来自农业径流、生活污水和城市雨水径流。
- 重金属：砷、镉、铅和汞等可来自地质背景及工业、人类活动。
- 微生物污染：大肠杆菌及其他病原体往往源于污水、化粪池和畜禽粪便。
- 新兴污染关注：微塑料、药品残留和个人护理品等。

--------------------------------------------------
4. Monitoring and Management
监测与管理
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Water-quality monitoring programs track nutrients, heavy metals, microbial indicators, and other parameters at multiple stations.
- Data are used to:
  - Assess compliance with guidelines
  - Detect long-term trends
  - Support policy decisions and management actions
- Collaboration among federal, provincial, and local agencies is critical for effective protection.

中文：
- 水质监测项目在多个站点采样，追踪营养盐、重金属、微生物指标以及其他参数。
- 这些数据可用于：
  - 评估是否符合各项指南标准
  - 识别长期变化趋势
  - 为政策制定和管理措施提供依据
- 联邦、省以及地方各级机构之间的合作对有效保护安大略湖水质十分关键。
