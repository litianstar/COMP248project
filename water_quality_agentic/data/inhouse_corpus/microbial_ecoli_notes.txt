Microbial Contamination and E. coli in Drinking Water
饮用水中的微生物污染与大肠杆菌

--------------------------------------------------
1. E. coli as an Indicator / 大肠杆菌作为指示菌
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Escherichia coli (E. coli) is widely used as an indicator of recent fecal contamination.
- If E. coli is detected in drinking water, it suggests that pathogens from human or animal waste may also be present.
- E. coli is more specific for fecal contamination than total coliforms.

中文：
- 大肠杆菌（E. coli）是饮用水质量监测中常用的“粪便污染指示菌”。
- 在饮用水样中检出大肠杆菌，说明可能存在来自人畜排泄物的病原体。
- 与总大肠菌群相比，大肠杆菌对粪便污染更具特异性。

--------------------------------------------------
2. WHO Guidelines for Microbial Safety
WHO 微生物安全指南
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- WHO guideline for E. coli in drinking water: 0 per 100 mL
- This means no E. coli should be detectable in any 100 mL sample of treated drinking water.
- For total coliforms, the presence in distribution systems is also undesirable and should be investigated.

中文：
- WHO 对饮用水中大肠杆菌的指南要求：每 100 mL 水样中不得检出（0 / 100 mL）。
- 这意味着在任何一份 100 mL 处理后的饮用水样中，都不应检测到大肠杆菌。
- 对于总大肠菌群，在供水管网中检出也需要进一步调查，并采取必要措施。

--------------------------------------------------
3. Health Risks / 健康风险
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Pathogens associated with fecal contamination include bacteria (e.g., pathogenic E. coli, Salmonella), viruses (e.g., norovirus), and protozoa (e.g., Giardia, Cryptosporidium).
- Symptoms may include diarrhea, vomiting, fever, and in severe cases, dehydration and hospitalization.

中文：
- 与粪便污染相关的病原体包括细菌（如致病性大肠杆菌、沙门氏菌）、病毒（如诺如病毒）以及原生动物（如贾第鞭毛虫、隐孢子虫）等。
- 感染后常见症状包括腹泻、呕吐、发热，严重时可导致脱水甚至需要住院治疗。

--------------------------------------------------
4. Sources of Microbial Contamination / 微生物污染的来源
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Inadequate disinfection of surface water sources
- Intrusion of contaminated surface water into wells
- Leaking septic tanks and sewage systems
- Animal access to water sources (e.g., livestock in streams)
- Heavy rainfall events that flush contaminants into surface and ground waters

中文：
- 地表水水源消毒不充分
- 受污染地表水渗入地下水井
- 渗漏的化粪池和污水管网
- 畜禽直接进入河流或水源地
- 强降雨过程将污染物冲刷进入地表水和地下水

--------------------------------------------------
5. Control Measures / 控制措施
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Maintain adequate disinfection (e.g., chlorination, UV disinfection).
- Protect wellheads and intake structures from surface runoff and animal access.
- Regularly inspect and maintain septic systems.
- Monitor E. coli and coliform counts, especially after heavy rainfall or flooding events.

中文：
- 保持适当的消毒措施（如氯消毒、紫外消毒等）。
- 保护井口和取水口，防止地表径流和畜禽进入。
- 定期检查和维护化粪池及相关污水处理设施。
- 在强降雨或洪涝之后，重点监测大肠杆菌和大肠菌群指标。
