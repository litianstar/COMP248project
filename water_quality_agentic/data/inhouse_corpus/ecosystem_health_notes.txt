Ecosystem Health Indicators in Lakes
湖泊生态健康的关键指标

--------------------------------------------------
1. Fish Communities / 鱼类群落
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Fish species composition, age structure, and health are important indicators of ecosystem status.
- Sensitive species may decline when water quality deteriorates or habitat is degraded.
- The presence of deformities, lesions, or tumors in fish can signal long-term contamination.

中文：
- 鱼类的种类组成、年龄结构和健康状况是湖泊生态系统状态的重要指标。
- 当水质恶化或栖息地受损时，敏感物种往往会减少或消失。
- 鱼体畸形、病变或肿瘤的出现可能提示长期污染的存在。

--------------------------------------------------
2. Phytoplankton and Zooplankton / 浮游植物与浮游动物
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Phytoplankton (algae) respond quickly to nutrient enrichment, especially phosphorus and nitrogen.
- Zooplankton graze on phytoplankton and link primary producers to higher trophic levels (fish).
- Changes in species composition (e.g., dominance of cyanobacteria) can indicate eutrophication.

中文：
- 浮游植物（藻类）对营养盐（特别是磷和氮）增加反应迅速。
- 浮游动物以浮游植物为食，是连接初级生产者和高营养级（如鱼类）的关键环节。
- 种群结构改变（如蓝藻占优势）往往提示水体富营养化。

--------------------------------------------------
3. Algal Blooms and Harmful Algal Blooms (HABs)
藻华与有害藻华
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Algal blooms occur when phytoplankton grow excessively due to high nutrient levels.
- Some blooms are dominated by cyanobacteria that can produce toxins (cyanotoxins).
- Harmful algal blooms (HABs) can affect drinking water safety, recreation, and aquatic life.

中文：
- 当营养盐水平升高时，浮游植物可能过度繁殖，形成大规模藻华。
- 部分藻华由蓝藻主导，可产生藻毒素（cyanotoxins）。
- 有害藻华会影响饮用水安全、休闲娱乐活动，并危害水生生物。

--------------------------------------------------
4. Physical-Chemical Indicators / 物理化学指标
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Key physical-chemical indicators of ecosystem health include:
  - Dissolved oxygen (DO)
  - Water temperature
  - pH
  - Nutrient concentrations (nitrogen, phosphorus)
  - Transparency (Secchi depth)
- Low dissolved oxygen in bottom waters can lead to fish kills and habitat loss.

中文：
- 描述湖泊生态健康的重要物理化学指标包括：
  - 溶解氧（DO）
  - 水温
  - pH 值
  - 营养盐浓度（氮、磷）
  - 透明度（Secchi 盘深度）
- 底层水体溶解氧偏低会导致鱼类死亡和栖息地丧失。

--------------------------------------------------
5. Integrated Assessment / 综合评估
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Ecosystem health is best assessed using multiple indicators rather than a single parameter.
- Combining biological (fish, plankton), chemical (nutrients, contaminants), and physical (temperature, DO) data provides a more complete picture.
- Long-term monitoring helps detect trends and evaluate the effectiveness of management actions.

中文：
- 评估湖泊生态健康不宜只依赖单一指标，而应综合多种生物、化学和物理指标。
- 将鱼类、浮游生物等生物学数据与营养盐、污染物及温度、溶解氧等物理化学数据结合，能更全面地反映生态状态。
- 长期监测有助于发现趋势变化，并评估管理措施的效果。
