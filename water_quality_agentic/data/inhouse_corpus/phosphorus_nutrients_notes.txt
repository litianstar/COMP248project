Phosphorus and Nutrients in Freshwater Systems
淡水系统中的磷与营养盐

--------------------------------------------------
1. Sources of Phosphorus / 磷的主要来源
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Phosphorus in lakes and rivers mainly comes from:
  - Agricultural fertilizer runoff
  - Livestock manure and storage lagoons
  - Domestic wastewater and detergents
  - Industrial discharges and food-processing effluents
- In rural areas, poorly managed septic systems can also contribute phosphorus to nearby surface waters.

中文：
- 湖泊和河流中的磷主要来源包括：
  - 农业化肥径流
  - 畜禽粪便及粪污储存设施渗漏
  - 生活污水和含磷洗涤剂
  - 工业排放和食品加工废水
- 在农村地区，管理不善的化粪池系统也可能向附近地表水体输送磷。

--------------------------------------------------
2. Role of Phosphorus in Eutrophication / 磷与富营养化
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Phosphorus is often the limiting nutrient in freshwater systems.
- When phosphorus inputs increase, algae and aquatic plants can grow excessively, leading to eutrophication.
- Eutrophication can cause:
  - Algal blooms (including harmful algal blooms)
  - Reduced water clarity
  - Oxygen depletion (hypoxia or anoxia) in bottom waters
  - Fish kills and loss of biodiversity

中文：
- 在大多数淡水系统中，磷往往是限制性营养元素。
- 当外源磷输入增加时，藻类和水生植物会过度生长，从而引发富营养化。
- 富营养化可能导致：
  - 大量藻华（包括有害藻华）
  - 水体透明度下降
  - 底层水体溶解氧降低甚至缺氧
  - 鱼类死亡和生物多样性下降

--------------------------------------------------
3. Ecological Impacts / 生态影响
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Persistent high phosphorus levels can shift the ecosystem from clear-water, plant-dominated states to turbid, algae-dominated states.
- Changes in species composition of phytoplankton and zooplankton can alter food webs and affect fish communities.
- Some cyanobacteria (blue-green algae) can produce toxins harmful to humans, pets, and wildlife.

中文：
- 持续高浓度的磷会使水体从“清澈、以大型水生植物为主”转变为“浑浊、以藻类为主”的状态。
- 浮游植物和浮游动物种群结构的变化会改变食物网，从而影响鱼类群落。
- 部分蓝藻（蓝绿藻）可产生对人类、宠物及野生动物有毒的藻毒素。

--------------------------------------------------
4. Management Strategies / 管理与控制措施
--------------------------------------------------
English:
- Reduce phosphorus application rates and adopt precision fertilization in agriculture.
- Maintain vegetated buffer strips along streams and lake shorelines to capture runoff.
- Upgrade wastewater treatment plants to remove phosphorus more efficiently.
- Promote phosphate-free detergents and better septic system maintenance in rural areas.

中文：
- 农业方面减少施肥量，并采用精准施肥技术。
- 在河岸和湖岸带建立植被缓冲带，拦截地表径流中的磷。
- 提高污水处理厂除磷工艺水平，提高去除效率。
- 在农村地区推广无磷洗涤剂，并加强化粪池系统的维护与管理。
